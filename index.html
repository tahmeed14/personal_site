About Me

I am currently a Data Scientist at KPMG US where I work in the Data & Analytics branch with the Signals Team.

Although my job title is “Data Scientist”, I was formally trained as a Statistician and I enjoy approaching problems from a “statistician’s perspective”. Outside of my professional life, I love to apply my knowledge of statistics to soccer (football) analytics and also dive deep into learning more about the tactical aspects of the sport.

My dad and cousin introduced me to soccer through the 2002 World Cup and I have followed it ever since. I have supported Chelsea F.C. since I was seven from the 2002/03 season and have supported the University of Michigan since my family and I moved to Ann Arbor in 2009.
